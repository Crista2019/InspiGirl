Interview: Sophie Paul

What school will you be attending next fall? If applicable, what will you be majoring in?

“Carnegie Mellon, Materials science & engineering”

What inspired you to venture into STEM? Was it a specific moment?

“My robotics team”

Tell us a fun fact about yourself

“I’m a certified Scuba Diver”

Who is your role model? Who do you most look up to and why?

“My robotics coach! She didn’t let me quit when things got hard, and she helped me come up with solutions myself.”

Describe a challenge you've faced as a woman in STEM, and how you've overcome it

"I joined a robotics team as a freshman. I was the only girl. All the boys got to do what they wanted, but I had to be the secretary. So I left and started an all girls team."

What is your favorite website, or app to use?

"Photoshop or iMovie"

Where do you see yourself in 10 years?

“I don’t know, but maybe working with wearable tech/advanced textiles”

What advice do you have for other InspiHERing women?

“Don’t give up!!”

Do you have a favorite quote or song lyrics that describes you/your journey?

“never the less, she persisted”

Please share any awards or recognitions you have received so we can highlight your accomplishments.

  • NCWIT Missouri Valley Affiliate Awardee
  • FIRST TECH CHALLENGE deans list semi finalist
  • Graduated high school top 10%
